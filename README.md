# Insurance Risk and Claims Analysis Dashboard
# Project Overview

Developed an interactive Insurance Risk and Claims Analysis Dashboard using Power BI to analyze insurance policy data, claim trends, customer demographics, and risk factors. The dashboard provides actionable insights into policy distribution, claim amounts, coverage zones, vehicle characteristics, and customer profiles to support data-driven decision-making in the insurance industry.

# Key Features
Analyzed 38K insurance policies and $187.8M total claim amount.
Monitored claim frequency and average claim amount across policyholders.
Visualized policy distribution by:
Car Use (Private vs Commercial)
Car Make and Model
Coverage Zone
Vehicle Year
Age Group
Education Level
Gender
Number of Kids Driving
Examined claim amounts based on Education and Marital Status.
Added dynamic filtering to enable interactive exploration of insurance data.

# Business Insights
Identified customer segments with higher policy concentrations.
Analyzed claim patterns across different demographic groups.

Supported insurance risk assessment and policy management decisions.
