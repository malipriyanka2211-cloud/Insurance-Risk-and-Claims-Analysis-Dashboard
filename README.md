# Insurance Risk and Claims Analysis Dashboard
# 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing insurance data using **Power BI**. The dashboard provides insights into policy distribution, claim amounts, customer demographics, vehicle characteristics, and coverage zones.

The goal of this project is to transform raw insurance data into meaningful visualizations that help identify risk patterns and support data-driven decision-making.

---

# 🎯 Business Objective

- Analyze the distribution of insurance policies across customer segments.
- Understand claim amounts and claim frequency.
- Compare policies by car make, car use, and coverage zone.
- Identify trends based on age groups and vehicle year.
- Analyze claim amounts by education and marital status.
- Explore demographic patterns across male and female customers.

# 📈 Key Insights

- Private car owners hold the majority of insurance policies.
- Ford and Chevrolet account for the highest number of policies.
- Policies are distributed almost equally across coverage zones.
- Customers aged 26–65 represent the largest share of policyholders.
- Claim amounts vary significantly based on education level and marital status.
- Male and female policyholders are nearly equal in number.
